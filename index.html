Hello!

Now that you've seen a bit of my work, here's a little bit about me!

I am a current Informatics student at the University of Washington, Seattle, as well as a Teaching Assistant for INFO 330, User Experience and Information Architecture, and INFO 102, Gender and Information Technology.

I am passionate about connecting people with technology. By exploring the intersection between technology, information, and people, I pride myself on the ability to mediate between technology and business professionals. I have over three years of experience in user experience, information architecture, web development, risk management, information systems, database management, information assurance, and cybersecurity.

When I am not in the classroom, I enjoy going to local theater productions (and having lively debates afterwards), drinking tea, playing tennis, skiing, and baking. (I make a mean cheesecake.)
