Who I Am

I am a Product Designer based in Seattle, WA. I currently design for BevyUp and revolutionize retail through personalization.

I am passionate about connecting people with technology. My education has taught me to explore the intersection between technology, information, and people. I pride myself on the ability to mediate between engineers, design, and product professionals.

When I am not in the office, I enjoy going to local theater productions (and having lively debates afterwards), drinking tea, playing tennis, skiing, wearing fuzzy socks, and baking. I make a mean cheesecake.
